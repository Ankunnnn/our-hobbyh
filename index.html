VOLLEYBALL

Volleyball, game played by two teams, usually of six players on a side, in which the players use their hands to bat a ball back and forth over a high net, trying to make the ball touch the court within the opponents' playing area before it can be returned.

TABLE TENNIS

Table tennis is also known as ping-pong and whiff-whaff. Two or four players hit a ball back and forth across a table using small solid rackets. Spinning the ball alters its trajectory and limits an opponent's options, giving the hitter a great advantage.
